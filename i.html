About Charity Run 2024

Organized by: The Health & Wellness Foundation, the Charity Run 2024 is a not-for-profit event aimed at raising funds and awareness for important social causes.

Date & Location: Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center - SECC, Hochiminh City, 11th October 2024.

Program Events - Charity Run 2024

Event Theme: Run for a Better Tomorrow

Charity Run 2024 carries the theme "Run for a Better Tomorrow," highlighting the importance of community spirit, health, and helping those in need. This event is more than just a race—it’s a platform for individuals to unite and contribute towards positive change in society.

Event Objectives

  • Raise Funds: All proceeds from the event will be directed to local charities that support education and healthcare for underprivileged communities.
  • Promote Health & Wellness: Encourage participants to lead active and healthy lifestyles through running and physical activities.
  • Build Community: Bring together diverse groups of people in support of a common cause, fostering a spirit of cooperation and solidarity.
  • Environmental Awareness: Promote sustainability by encouraging eco-friendly practices throughout the event.

Schedule of Activities

Time Event Location
6:00 AM - 7:00 AM Registration & Warm-up Session Main Stage
7:30 AM Start of 5K Run Running Track
8:00 AM Start of 10K Run Running Track
9:30 AM - 12:00 PM Charity Expo & Wellness Workshops Exhibition Hall
12:30 PM Closing Ceremony & Awards Main Stage

Featured Activities

  • 5K Run: A fun and easy run for beginners and families, perfect for enjoying the outdoors and supporting a cause.
  • 10K Run: For experienced runners looking for a challenge, this race will test endurance and speed.
  • Charity Expo: Explore booths from local businesses and non-profits focused on health, wellness, and sustainable living.
  • Wellness Workshops: Free workshops on fitness, nutrition, and mindfulness led by experts in the field.
  • Live Entertainment: Enjoy music performances and entertainment throughout the day.
  • Eco-Friendly Initiatives: A special focus on reducing waste and promoting recycling during the event.

Additional Information

Participants will receive a race kit that includes a Charity Run 2024 T-shirt, race bib, and a medal for completing the race. Water stations will be available throughout the track, and medical staff will be on standby for any emergencies.

General Information - Charity Run 2024

Venue Information

The Charity Run 2024 will be held at the Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center (SECC), located in Hochiminh City. This modern venue is equipped with excellent facilities to accommodate participants and spectators.

How to Reach the Venue

Address:

799 Nguyễn Văn Linh, Tân Phú, Quận 7, Hồ Chí Minh, Việt Nam

Transportation Options:

  • By Car: The SECC is easily accessible by car with ample parking space available on-site.
  • By Public Transport: You can take the Metro Line 1 (Ben Thanh - Suoi Tien) and get off at the nearest station. Several bus routes also serve the area.
  • Ride-Sharing: Services like Grab and Uber are widely available in the city and can drop you off directly at the venue.

Parking Facilities

The venue provides designated parking areas for participants and attendees. Parking is available free of charge for all event-goers.

Event Participants

Exhibitors:

Partners & Sponsors:

We are proud to partner with various local and national sponsors who contribute to the success of Charity Run 2024. Their support helps us to maximize our fundraising efforts.

Special Entitlements for Participants

All participants in the Charity Run 2024 will receive:

Our Philosophy:
